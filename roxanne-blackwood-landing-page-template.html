Mr. Taquito Welcomes You!

Got a craving? We got you!

Mr. Taquito is a family-owned business serving up crave-worthy tacos, burritos, tortas & more!

Established in 2020, two childhood friends from C-Town turned their dreams of building a casual Cali-Mex dining experience into a reality using original recipes crafted by co-owner Jake Zuniga and his mother Melinda, hailing from Colima, Mexico.
